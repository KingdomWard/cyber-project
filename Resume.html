Cyber Obscurity

According to the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center,
more than 5,000 people living in South Carolina
had made complaints about cyber crimes in 2022.

There are many companies and practices that are exposed to these
crimes at any given moment. Americans have lost billions of
dollars due to cyber crimes.

This web page helps illustrate different types of cyberattacks that can happen
to anyone. For those who don't know much about cyber attacks,
this web page may be helpful.

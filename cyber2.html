Cyber Attacks

Malware

There are a lot of types of malware. Some include but are not limited to:

Viruses
Trojans
RansomWare
Adware
Cryptominers
Rootkits

 These types of malware can do a lot of harm like using locking your files
and making you pay a fee to unlock them, using your computer to mine cryptocurrency,
or malicious code that won't execute until a certain time or date.

Ransomware has been increasingly popular over the years and has been another one of the most
common hacks to occur. A lot of these ransomware hacks encrypt files with a virus and
the attackers demand a huge sum of money in return in order for the files to be decrypted.

In April of 2019, the City of Greenville, NC suffered a ransomware attack through their government
systems and networks. The attackers used ransomware called RobbinHood which uses a Remote Desktop Protocol
to cross over to other computers. The City did end up paying the ransom and had to go through a long and
expensive recovery process.

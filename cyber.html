Cyber Attacks

Phishing

One of the most common attacks and is increasingly getting
worse, phishing involves the use of acquiring personal data like
passwords, credit card numbers, social security numbers, etc.
through means of scamming through email, text, or other
methods. A convincing email from 'amazon' asking you to cancel
an order that has already charged your card, or a phone call from
your bank asking you to verify using your SSN are a few examples.
These attacks are becoming more common and anyone can be a victim.

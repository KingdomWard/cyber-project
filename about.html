About me

Hey, I'm John. I go to school at USC Upstate in Spartanburg, SC
and I've been learning so much about the tech industry and I can't
get enough. Programming has always been interesting to me and it always
seems like there is endless knowledge when learning about engineering, but
cybersecurity has become more interesting to me every day.

Cybersecurity has always been a huge part of the tech industry and the world
in general. As technology advances to become faster and smarter, there are individuals
who want to take advantage of businesses and normal people by infiltrating and
exposing their private information, stealing resources, demanding money, etc. As people
start to lose assets and jobs, attackers become more motivated to enable cyber warfare against
those that have no idea how to stop or prevent a cyber attack.

Helping those that don't have the knowledge seems like a good start in helping to alleviate
the problem but I know that more has to be done in order to fully protect people who may be
vulnerable to cyber attacks. I hope to become a big part in helping those who can't defend themselves
by gaining more knowledge and experience in engineering and Information Technology.

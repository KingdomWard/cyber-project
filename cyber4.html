Cyber Attacks


Keyloggers


A keylogger is a program that records all of your keystrokes, even though
you might not be aware of it. This can infect your computer by clicking on
suspicious links or downloading files from the internet. The program will run
in the background if not detected by your anti-virus software and will write them all to
to a secret .txt file where a hacker can then read and see what passwords you input
or any other sensitive information that you type with your keyboard.
